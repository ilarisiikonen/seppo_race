🍺 SEPPO LAST RACE 🏎️

The debt collector showed up at Seppo's door. An old friend, sent to settle what was owed. Words turned to shouts. Shouts turned to something worse.

When it was over, his friend lay still on the kitchen floor. Seppo stood there, hands shaking, knowing nothing could undo what he'd done.

He grabbed his keys and drove. On a dark stretch of road, a figure stepped out of nowhere. The car hit them before Seppo could even think. He didn't stop.

Now blue lights flash in the rearview mirror. The sirens are getting closer. There is only the road ahead.
